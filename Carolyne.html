New Name, Who dis? Also, Milestone 1...COMMENCE!

You may or may not have noticed, we changed our group name. After much thought, we decided Automated Hydroponics System is well, 1. Too literal, it doesn't sound like anything and 2. It's lame, where's the flavour?! We will now go by our new name,PiFarm(pi 'cause the raspberry pi and automation and farm since... hydroponic farming, we're so clever :))

Preparation of Milestone 1 has begun, General information has been added... not much to talk about. Will discussed the remaining headers I didn't fill out with Stef and Zane over the weekend. We'll be able to start thinking more in depth of like interface and all that.

Sidenote(kinda): This week's lecture was very informative and enjoyable, we did "Chosing the Right database" in class today. After some thought and discussion, we kind of decided on the frameworks and databases, React.js and Firestone Firebase. We need that real-time.
